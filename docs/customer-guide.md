# Welcome to Savaal Guide

Your guide to discovering exceptional hospitality across Southern Africa.

---

## What is Savaal Guide?

Savaal Guide is a curated hospitality platform that celebrates excellence in African hospitality. We feature hand-selected restaurants, bars, and hotels across Southern Africa, each assessed by professional judges using a transparent scoring system.

Whether you're looking for a fine dining experience, a standout bar, or a quality stay, Savaal Guide helps you discover venues that meet the highest standards of hospitality.

---

## How to Navigate the Site

### The Guide

**Where to find it:** Click **"Guide"** in the top navigation bar, or visit `/guide`.

The Guide is the heart of Savaal. Here you'll find our curated directory of restaurants, bars, and hotels.

**Browsing the Guide:**

- **Search** - Use the search bar at the top to find venues by name.
- **Filter by Region** - Narrow results to a specific geographic area.
- **Filter by Category** - Choose between Restaurants, Stays (Hotels), or view all.
- **Filter by Price Level** - Select venues that match your budget (1 = budget-friendly, 5 = luxury).
- **Filter by Distinction** - View only Savaal Star or Savaal Selected venues.

**Viewing a Venue:**

Click on any venue card to see its full detail page, which includes:

- **Overview** - Description, location, cuisine type, and price level.
- **Scores** - A breakdown of how the venue scored across our 7 judging categories.
- **Judge Comments** - What our judges had to say about their experience.
- **Photo Gallery** - Images of the venue, food, and ambiance.
- **Menu** - Selected menu items with descriptions and pricing (where available).

---

### Understanding Our Ratings

Every venue in the Guide has been assessed by professional judges using our 100-point scoring system.

**The 7 Scoring Categories:**

| Category | What It Measures |
|----------|-----------------|
| Taste & Technique | Quality of food and culinary skill |
| Service | Attentiveness, professionalism, and warmth of service |
| Beverage Experience | Quality and range of drinks |
| Menu Composition | Variety, balance, and creativity of the menu |
| Presentation | How food and drinks are presented |
| Ambience | Atmosphere, decor, and overall feel |
| Perceived Value | Whether the experience justifies the price |

**Savaal Distinctions:**

- **Savaal Star** - Venues that have achieved the highest levels of excellence. Stars range from 1 to 3, with 3 being the pinnacle.
- **Savaal Selected** - Quality venues that have been recognized by Savaal for meeting our standards of hospitality.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How are venues selected for the Guide?

Venues are assessed by professional judges using Savaal's 100-point scoring system across 7 categories. Only venues that meet our standards are listed.

### What's the difference between Savaal Star and Savaal Selected?

**Savaal Star** venues have achieved exceptional scores and represent the highest tier of hospitality excellence (1-3 stars). **Savaal Selected** venues have been recognized as meeting Savaal's quality standards.

### How can my business get rated by Savaal?

Visit the **Judging > For Businesses** page to learn about the assessment process, or contact us through the Contact page.

### How do I become a Savaal judge?

Visit the **Judging > For Prospective Judges** page to learn about qualifications and how to apply.

### How can I attend a Savaal event?

Browse upcoming events on the **Events** page. Each event listing includes details on how to book or register.

### How do I get in touch with Savaal?

Use the **Contact** page or click the "Get in Touch" button in the navigation bar. You can also follow us on Instagram (linked in the footer).
